Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

NearMe ("we", "us", or "the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

Location data. With your permission, NearMe accesses your device's location to show events happening near you. Your precise location is used only on-device to query nearby events from our servers. We do not store your location history.

Preferences. Your selected interests, tags, search radius, saved events, and custom locations are stored locally on your device. This data is not synced to our servers unless you sign in to a future account feature.

Analytics. We may collect anonymous usage data (app opens, feature usage, crashes) through standard mobile analytics. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you individually.

Payment data. If you subscribe to NearMe Premium, payment is processed by Apple via the App Store. We never see or store your payment information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To show you relevant events near your location
  • To personalize your feed based on your selected interests
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To process your subscription (via Apple)

Third-Party Services

NearMe uses the following third-party services. Each has their own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — backend infrastructure for event data
  • Apple StoreKit — subscription processing
  • Google Maps — map display
  • Expo — app infrastructure

Events displayed in the app are sourced from public APIs including Ticketmaster, SeatGeek, Google Places, and public venue websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers or marketing companies. We may share anonymized, aggregated statistics publicly (e.g., "NearMe users saved 10,000 events this month").

Your Rights

You can:

  • Turn off location permission at any time in iPhone Settings
  • Reset your preferences via the "Reset App" button in Settings
  • Request deletion of any server-side data by emailing us
  • Cancel your subscription via iPhone Settings → Subscriptions

Children's Privacy

NearMe is not intended for users under 17 due to event content that may include bars, nightlife, and alcohol-related activities. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Significant changes will be communicated in-app. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
