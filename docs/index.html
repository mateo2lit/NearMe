FAQ

How does NearMe find events?

We aggregate events from Ticketmaster, SeatGeek, local venue websites, and public city calendars. Events are updated continuously.

Does NearMe work in my city?

Yes — NearMe works anywhere in the US (and growing). Set a custom address in Settings or let the app use your GPS to find events around you.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → NearMe → Cancel. Your subscription stays active until the end of the billing period.

Is my data safe?

Yes. NearMe only stores what's needed to serve you events. We don't sell your data, track you across apps, or share personal info with third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

I don't see any events near me — help?

First load in a new area can take 30-60 seconds while we sync data. Try expanding your search radius in Settings. If it still shows empty, email us and we'll look into it.

Can I report an incorrect event?

Absolutely. Email us with the event name and what's wrong, and we'll fix it.
