NearMe Privacy Policy

Effective: May 1, 2026

NearMe ("we", "us", "the app") is an event-discovery app that helps you find things to do near you. This policy describes what we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We try to keep it short and plain.

What we collect

  • Location — your approximate or precise location, used only to find events and venues near you. You can deny location access; the app will fall back to a default region.
  • Onboarding preferences — the goals, vibe, schedule, social style, blocker, and budget you select during setup, plus any categories or tags you toggle later. We use these to rank and filter events for you.
  • Activity inside the app — events you save, hide, or open. We use this to improve your feed and prevent duplicates.
  • Anonymous user ID — a random UUID stored on your device. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, contacts, or social accounts.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active NearMe Pro subscription. Apple processes the payment; we receive only the entitlement status, not your payment details.

How we use it

  • To find and rank events that match your preferences and location.
  • To remember what you've saved or hidden across sessions.
  • To keep your subscription unlocked across devices that share your Apple ID.
  • To diagnose crashes and improve the app.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for targeted ads. NearMe does not track you across other apps or websites.

Service providers we use

  • Supabase — stores your preferences, anonymous user ID, and saved/hidden events on our backend.
  • RevenueCat — manages your subscription state and tells the app whether your trial or subscription is active.
  • Apple App Store — processes all payments and trial signups. We never see your card details.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — receives a small, anonymized payload (your preferences and a list of nearby events) on our server to help rank what to show you. No personally identifying information is sent.

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and only receive what they need to run their part of the service.

Data retention

We keep your preferences and activity for as long as you use NearMe. If you delete the app, your local data is removed from your device. To delete the data we store on our backend, email us and we'll remove your record within 30 days.

Your rights

  • Access or delete your data — email us at dbhershman@gmail.com with the subject "NearMe data request".
  • Manage your subscription — at any time in Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your device.
  • Revoke location access — at any time in Settings → Privacy → Location Services.

Children

NearMe is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Material changes will also be announced inside the app.
